Frequently Asked Questions

Don't See Your Question Here? Reach Out to Us! We're Here to Help.

How can I be certain the driver will arrive?

Our drivers will send you a message on the day to confirm the time, vehicle and any registration involved!

What payment methods can I use?

We take payment the day before using an online payment processor or alternatively the driver can accept cash or card on the day.

Note: A standard charge £2.50 is applied for all card payments as a processing fee.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

No worries! Our drivers have live flight trackers to ensure safe pickup. The driver will arrange the new time and location of the pick-up with you and you won't be charged.

We need multiple drop-offs, how does that work?

Not a problem, any extra drop-offs will cost £5 each.
